All-terrain vehicle chassis

A stiff, light shell

For an all terrain vehicle the chassis has to be torsionally stiff so the suspension behaves predictably, yet light enough to be quick. I engineered a high torsional stiffness monocoque chassis built to BAJA SAE standards, balancing structural integrity against mass optimization.

Cornering and power

I implemented an anti Ackermann steering geometry to tune the tyre slip angles and sharpen high intensity off road cornering, and ran iterative kinematic and stress analyses on an integrated, high efficiency geartrain to keep the power transmission robust under dynamic loading.
