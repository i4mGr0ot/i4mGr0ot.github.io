5-DOF Manipulator
Reconfigurable robotics

One arm, many jobs

I designed and prototyped a five degree of freedom robotic manipulator with a hot swappable end effector interface, so a single arm can switch between very different jobs without being rebuilt.

From microscope to warehouse

A rotational base architecture and the control logic let the arm move between tasks that range from microscopic sample acquisition to warehouse automation. I analysed the workspace envelope and the kinematic constraints to tune the end effector precision for both high fidelity research and heavier industrial use.
