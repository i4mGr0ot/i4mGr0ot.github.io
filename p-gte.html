Controlling a jet engine

A Full Authority Digital Engine Controller is the brain of a jet engine, the unit that schedules fuel and protects the engine across its whole operating envelope. I am building a modular FADEC for turbojet engines on an STM32 Nucleo H743ZI2.

Test it before you burn fuel

To develop and tune the controller without risking hardware, I built a C++ software in the loop framework that simulates the turbojet, so the FADEC logic can be exercised against a virtual engine first.

Hybrid powertrain

On the propulsion side I worked on a hybrid UAV powertrain that pairs microturbojets with electric ducted fans on a 130 kilogram model aircraft, blending the endurance of a turbine with the responsiveness of electric thrust.
