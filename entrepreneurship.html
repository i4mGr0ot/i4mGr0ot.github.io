Entrepreneurship

Taking ideas to market.

As Chief Technical Officer I have led the engineering on two ventures, one reaching toward lunar resources and one cleaning the air around us, both about turning hard physics into something real and fundable.

AtmosClear
Smart Autonomous Air Purification
Chief Technical Officer · Dec 2024 to Feb 2026

AtmosClear

I designed and developed a working model of a state of the art smart autonomous mobile air purifier for household and industrial use. The product moves itself to where air quality is worst and cleans it, rather than waiting passively in a corner.

The design is presently under review for a design patent, and the venture has been funded through the Nidhi Prayas programme of the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India.

Space-Mined
In-Situ Resource Utilisation
Chief Technical Officer · Jun 2025 to Oct 2025

Space-Mined

I worked on a space mission design for laser induced thermal processing of lunar regolith, extracting metal and oxygen in situ for highly efficient fuel conversion. The idea is to make future missions live off the land rather than carry everything up the gravity well.

Working with a Luxembourg startup accelerator, I helped develop a semi scale prototype toward a TRL 3 version of the concept.

Why I do this

Research teaches me to be rigorous, but building a venture teaches me everything the equations leave out, manufacturing, cost, regulation, and the stubborn gap between a prototype and a product. I like living in that gap.

Both roles pushed me to lead small technical teams, make trade offs under real constraints, and pitch the work to people holding the cheque. That combination is exactly the kind of integration I want to keep doing.
