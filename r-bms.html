The setup

This is a coupled electrochemical and thermal model of a high power battery energy storage system, an eight module, 14S4P liquid cooled pack drawn from the Pininfarina Battista electric hypercar. The cell model carries the ohmic, activation and concentration losses, which enter the heat equation as sources, and the cooling is modelled with nonisothermal turbulent and pipe flow in COMSOL Multiphysics.

What it shows

The full pack temperature field at the top of the page holds the spread to roughly twelve degrees between the coolest and hottest cells. That kind of cell to cell uniformity is exactly what a high discharge, fast charging pack needs to stay safe and to age evenly.
